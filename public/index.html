About

I'm a Cloud & Infrastructure Solutions Architect based in the Greater Toronto Area. For over 14 years, I've worked with Fortune 500 clients, banks, and global enterprises — building, migrating, and running the infrastructure that lets engineering teams focus on shipping product.

I've led teams of up to 8 engineers across financial services, telecom, automotive, insurance, and enterprise tech — designing cloud platforms, driving IaC (Terraform), taming CI/CD pipelines, and turning infrastructure costs into strategic advantages. Comfortable owning things end-to-end from pre-sales solutioning through to delivery.
